Welcome to Pylos!

The game will operate by prompting you for a move when required
To make a move, enter precisely in the following format your desired move:

To place a sphere, please enter:

place [position]

Where position identifies the position you’d like to place the sphere
Coordinates should be a letter (identifying the row) followed by a number (identifying the column)

Example: place a4


To raise a sphere, please enter:

raise [source] [destination]

Where source and destination are specified in the above format

Example: raise a1 i2

LEGEND: 
‘E’ stands for empty (i.e. no sphere)
‘W’ stands for white sphere
‘B’ stands for black sphere
